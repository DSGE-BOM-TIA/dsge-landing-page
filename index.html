About DSGE

Built as a deployment-ready operating system

DSGE is being developed as a practical circular deployment model for recovery, traceable material flow, stakeholder coordination, workforce advancement, and measurable value creation.

The goal is to help organizations move from fragmented disposal-driven behavior toward structured recovery, resilience, and scalable economic impact.

Why it matters

Preparedness, clarity, and measurable value

Organizations are under pressure to reduce cost, improve reporting, respond faster, and build stronger workforce and partner ecosystems. DSGE is designed to support all four.

Mission

Deploy practical circular systems

Convert waste, underused material streams, and operational friction into measurable economic, environmental, and community value.

Vision

Build a scalable regional model

Help facilities, partners, institutions, and communities operate with stronger visibility, faster deployment capability, workforce readiness, and long-term circular resilience.

Careers, not jobs

Long-term advancement pathways

DSGE is structured around capability building. The intent is not just to fill positions, but to create visible advancement tied to field readiness, technology fluency, discipline, and leadership growth.

  • Entry pathway: field support, sorting, documentation, logistics assistance
  • Skilled pathway: equipment, quality, recovery coordination, reporting support
  • Technical pathway: data systems, workflow automation, traceability support, R&D assistance
  • Leadership pathway: site lead, deployment coordinator, partner manager, program supervisor
