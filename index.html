About Us

DSGE, LLC is building a practical operating model that helps organizations see, measure, and act on circularity, workforce development, and local economic value.

The goal is not only to inform stakeholders, but to give them a usable path to engage, pilot, partner, train, and scale.

Who this page is for

  • Industry partners and site hosts
  • Workforce development organizations
  • Universities, vocational schools, and R&D collaborators
  • Public and private stakeholders evaluating deployment readiness

Mission

To create transparent, practical systems that turn resource, workforce, and sustainability challenges into measurable operational, economic, and community value.

Mission

To create transparent, practical systems that turn resource, workforce, and sustainability challenges into measurable operational, economic, and community value.

Vision

A future where circular operations, measurable impact, workforce pathways, and cross-sector collaboration are easy to understand, easy to verify, and easy to scale.

Values

Transparency

Make systems, requirements, and outcomes visible enough for stakeholders to understand and act on.

Practical Action

Focus on usable workflows, measurable milestones, and deployment-ready solutions rather than vague promises.

Shared Value

Build models where partners, operators, communities, and learners can all benefit from successful execution.
